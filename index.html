Service Details & Deliverables

📈 Global Market Analysis (S001)

What's included: Daily market briefings (email/portal), weekly in-depth analysis reports, monthly market outlook presentations, ad-hoc market event analysis, and access to our proprietary market data platform.

Deliverables: Customizable dashboards, PDF reports with data visualizations, video conference briefings (monthly), and 24/7 access to our analyst team for urgent market queries.

Pricing model: Monthly subscription (Starter: £499/month, Professional: £999/month, Enterprise: Custom pricing)

💼 Personal Asset Planning (S002)

What's included: Comprehensive financial health assessment, personalized asset allocation strategy, tax optimization planning, retirement planning, and regular portfolio reviews (quarterly).

Deliverables: Custom financial plan document, portfolio allocation recommendations, retirement projection models, and ongoing strategy adjustments as needed.

Pricing model: Initial consultation fee (£350) + annual retainer (£1,500-£5,000 based on portfolio size)

🏢 Institutional Research Service (S003)

What's included: Custom industry research reports, macroeconomic forecasting, competitor analysis, investment opportunity identification, and white-label research for your clients.

Deliverables: Custom research reports, interactive data dashboards, presentation materials, and analyst support for client meetings.

Pricing model: Project-based pricing (£5,000-£50,000) or annual research retainer (£50,000-£250,000)

🛡️ Financial Risk Assessment (S004)

What's included: Comprehensive portfolio risk analysis, stress testing against market scenarios, liquidity risk assessment, credit risk evaluation, and risk mitigation strategies.

Deliverables: Risk assessment report with mitigation recommendations, scenario analysis models, and ongoing risk monitoring (monthly).

Pricing model: One-time assessment (£2,500) or ongoing monitoring retainer (£800/month)

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions our clients ask about our services, pricing, and processes.

What makes KEYU different from other financial consulting firms? ▼

KEYU combines academic rigor with practical market experience, offering customized solutions rather than one-size-fits-all advice. Our team includes former senior executives from top global financial institutions, and we maintain full transparency in our fee structure and research methodologies. Unlike many firms, we don't accept commissions or kickbacks from financial products, ensuring our advice is always in your best interest.

How much do your consulting services cost? ▼

Our pricing varies based on the service type, scope, and client size. Personal asset planning starts at £350 for an initial consultation with annual retainers from £1,500. Institutional services are customized based on specific needs, typically ranging from £5,000 for single projects to £250,000 for annual retainers. We offer a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your needs and provide a detailed quote with no obligation.

Do you work with individual investors or only institutions? ▼

We work with both individual investors (high-net-worth and mass affluent) and institutional clients including hedge funds, asset managers, corporations, and family offices. Our services are scaled to meet the specific needs of each client type, with dedicated teams for individual and institutional clients to ensure specialized attention.

How quickly can I expect to receive analysis or reports? ▼

Daily market briefings are delivered by 8:00 AM GMT, weekly reports by Friday close, and monthly outlooks by the 5th of each month. Custom research projects typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity, with urgent requests (24-48 hours) available for an additional fee. Personal financial plans are delivered within 2 weeks of receiving all necessary client information.

Is my financial information secure with KEYU? ▼

We take data security extremely seriously and maintain the highest standards of confidentiality. All client data is encrypted and stored on secure servers in the UK, compliant with GDPR and FCA regulations. We never share client information with third parties without explicit consent, and all team members sign strict non-disclosure agreements. Our security protocols are audited annually by independent cybersecurity firms.
